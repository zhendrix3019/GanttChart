3. Key Implementation Details
Vector Content: For architectural drawings, use SVG or HTML5 Canvas instead of standard images. SVGs are vector-based and will print sharply at any resolution (24x36 or 11x17) without pixelation.
Handling the 50% Scale (11" x 17"): An Arch D sheet (24x36) is exactly twice the size of a tabloid sheet (11x17) in terms of aspect ratio. If you print your 24x36 master PDF or web page and select "Fit to Page" in the browser's print dialog, it will scale perfectly to 11x17 without you needing extra code.
Resolution: Ensure your CSS pixels are mapped correctly. At 96 DPI (standard for CSS), a 24" x 36" sheet is 2304px x 3456px.
